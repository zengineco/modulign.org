## What Modulign does

A Modulign address encodes:

- **What** was observed (domain, subdomain, activity state)
- **Where** it was observed (realm, geo hierarchy to four levels, node)
- **At what scale** it was observed (quantum to cosmological)
- **By whom** (observer type, domain competence, automated vs. human vs. hybrid)
- **When** (ISO 8601 timestamp)
- **Under what legal framework** (jurisdiction axis)
- **With what epistemic status** (^EVID, ^PROV, ^VFYD)
- **In what causal relationship** to other observations (Relation Registry)

A minimum valid address takes seconds to produce. A full ^EVID-grade classification satisfies legal admissibility requirements across all major legal systems without additional documentation.
